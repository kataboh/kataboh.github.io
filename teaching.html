Ingredients for an Effective Teacher
  • Show compassion
  • Make sure your students are heard, included, and not alone
  • Be enthusiastic!
  • Prepare ahead of time
  • Take charge
  • Connect the mathematics to the real world
  • Acknowledge when you don't have an answer
